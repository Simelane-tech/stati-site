Exciting Highlights from the Sibeve Group Golf Competition at Mbabane Golf Club

October 11, 2025 5 min read
← Back to Blog

What happens when Eswatini's premier branding company sponsors a day of golf at one of the country's most scenic courses? The answer: a spectacular blend of competition, community, and celebration that no one will soon forget.

A Day of Golf, Glory, and Giving Back

On the beautiful day of October 11, 2025, the Mbabane Golf Club came alive with the thrilling annual golf competition sponsored by the Sibeve Group. Golf enthusiasts gathered to partake in a day filled with friendly competition, camaraderie, and excitement as they aimed to showcase their skills on the course.

The event was not just about the golf; it was a celebration of sportsmanship, with prizes galore including stunning trophies, stylish t-shirts, high-quality bags, refreshing bottles, and much more. To support the game, Sibeve provided snacks, refreshments, and lunch that energized players throughout the day, ensuring that everyone was well-supported and ready to take on the challenges of the course.

Winners of the Day

The competition saw an impressive display of talent, with participants vying for the top spots. Here are the distinguished winners of the tournament:

Men's Category

  1. King Mauda — Nett score of 70, clinching the coveted first place!
  2. Sifiso Mashwama — A phenomenal performance with a score of 72 (count back), securing second place.
  3. Ncamiso Dlamini — Tied with a score of 72, showcasing remarkable consistency.
  4. Lwazi Mchobokazi — Also scoring 72, proving to be a fierce competitor.
  5. Albert Mhlanga — Closing strong with a score of 74.

Ladies' Category

  1. Nelly Mathabela — Dominated the ladies' leaderboard with a fantastic performance!
  2. Nolly Ginindza — Followed closely, earning her position with great play.

Novelty Prizes

  • Nearest to the Pin (2/11): Behlule Dlomo
  • Ladies' Longest Drive: Nelly Mathabela
  • Nearest to the Pin (8/17): King Coach
  • Longest Putt: Meshack Zwane
  • Longest Drive: Sifiso Mashwama

A Day to Remember

This year's competition showcased not only the skills of the players but also the spirit of togetherness in the golfing community. The lively atmosphere, combined with the stunning scenery of Mbabane Golf Club, made for an unforgettable experience for all participants and spectators alike.

As we celebrate the achievements of our talented golfers, the Sibeve Group extends heartfelt congratulations to all the winners and participants. Your dedication and enthusiasm truly enhance the experience of the event.

Thank You to Our Supporters

A special thank you goes out to our sponsors and supporters who made this event possible. Your commitment to promoting golf and community involvement in Mbabane is greatly appreciated. We look forward to seeing everyone at our next competition!

Stay tuned for more updates from the Sibeve Group, and keep practicing those swings!
